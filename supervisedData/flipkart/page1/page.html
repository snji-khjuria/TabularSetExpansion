    Product Description
    Flaunt your style with this Smartron t.phone P smartphone which features a premium full metal body and impressive features. Powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 435 octa-core processor and 3 GB of RAM, this smartphone provides you with a fluid experience while running apps or playing games. The 32 GB internal memory which is expandable up to 128 GB provides ample space for your pictures, videos, music, and games.
    Incredibly Fast

    Powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 435 octa-core processor, the Qualcomm Adreno 505 GPU and 3 GB of RAM, this smartphone ensures that your apps and games load quickly without much lag.

    Expandable Internal Memory

    Dont worry about deleting your old pictures and videos to make room for new ones, as this smartphone comes with a 32 GB internal memory which can be expandable up to 128 GB. Also, it comes with 1000 GB free t-cloud storage.

    Capture Your Precious Moments

    Take stunning pictures and beautiful selfies with the 13 MP rear camera and the 5 MP front camera. Experiment with different shooting modes such as beautify, panorama, time lapse, multi-exposure, and burst. Take group selfies without cutting out anyone with the Wide Selfie mode even in poor lighting conditions, thanks to the front and rear LED flashes.

    Long Battery Life

    The fun never ends, as this smartphone comes with a massive 5000 mAh battery which lasts for a long time. It also features the OTG Reverse Charge Technology which lets you charge other USB devices like another smartphone. The phone stays on even after you watch YouTube videos, listen to music, browse the Internet, and make voice calls.

    Premium Full Metal Body

    The 13.2 cm (5.2) HD display lets you watch your favourite movies in impeccable clarity. This ergonomically-designed smartphone also features a 2.5D curved edge which allows the phone to slide easily into your pocket.

    Quick Fingerprint Sensor

    Besides improving security, it also helps you quickly unlock your phone. It also lets you take pictures and selfies, and answer calls with just a gentle tap on the sensor.
