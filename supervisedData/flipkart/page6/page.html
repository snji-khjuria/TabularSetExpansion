    Need a sound system for your party? Get all the Samsung GALAXY S4 phones together and create a powerful sound system that enhances the sound quality. You can also wirelessly connect multiple Samsung Galaxy S4 phones to play games and share photos and documents.

    You've taken too many pictures and you don't want them to get mixed up. Samsung Galaxy S4 organises your photos to create customised albums for every occasion choosing various layouts.

It allows tourists to communicate with the locals, helps discover foreign dishes and explore hidden hangouts. Languages supported are English, German, French, Chinese, Spanish, Italian, Japanese and Korean.

    Air View

    Barely hover your finger over the screen to get a quick preview, enlarge photos or speed dial. Also, you can control your phone by just waving your hand over the screen to accept calls or to browse.

    When you look away while watching a video, this smartphone detects your moves and pauses the video so you don't miss anything. Look back at the screen again to resume your video. This phone intuitively moves along with you and scrolls up or down when you tilt the phone from one side to another.

    Samsung Home Sync

    Home Sync is your personal cloud device with 1 TB of storage capacity that can store tons of pictures, videos and games. Stream content directly on your TV and navigate with ease with Mirror Mouse, a specialised navigating feature of Samsung Galaxy S4.

    Samsung WatchON

    S4 becomes a TV remote when you connect it with your home entertainment system, so it suggest you programs that you may like, gives you program schedules and even does channel surfing for you.

    S Health

    With S Health feature, you can keep track of your workouts, daily intake and weight levels. It also shows your comfort levels based on temperature and humidity outside.

    Adapt Display & Sound

    Adapt Display adjusts your view with 7 automatic modes and 4 manual modes so as to give your eyes a rest. Adapt Sound gives you the perfect volume customised just for you by dialing music up and down and balancing left and right audio based on your hearing.
