    Product Description
    Panasonic brings you the Eluga I5 smartphone, a phone that’s designed to adapt to you. It packs ARBO, the artificial intelligence system which tracks your daily routine and makes schedules for you. The phone’s metal body is sleek, and easily fits your pocket. The fingerprint sensor securely locks away your phone’s contents.
    See Bigger, See Clearer

    Its 12.7 cm (5) HD IPS display, with Asahi Dragontrail glass brings you clear and sharp pictures.

    Capture More

    The 13 MP rear camera packs the LED flash burst mode which lets you click many pictures at one go. Its 5 MP front camera lets you indulge your selfie-loving self.

    Fast Performance

    A 1.25 GHz quad-core processor, with a 2 GB of RAM, powers this phone to offer you a smooth user experience.

    Expansive Internal Memory

    Apps, games, pictures, and other media files are saved in its 16 GB inbuilt memory, which is expandable up to an additional 128 GB via a microSD card.

    Do More

    The Android Nougat 7.0 OS facilitates smooth multitasking. The split screen feature lets you work on two different apps at the same time.

    ARBO

    This artificial intelligence system is designed to understand you and your life, and help you live every day to the fullest. It tracks your routine and accordingly recommends schedules for you.

    Long-lasting Battery

    Its 2500 mAh battery lasts almost an entire day, on a single full charge.

    Designed to Please

    Its 7.5 mm-slim metal body looks sleek and is easy to hold.

    Fingerprint Sensor

    The inbuilt fingerprint sensor lets you unlock your phone with just one touch. It also lets you scroll through pages and apps with ease.
